About the project

Imagine finding a magical valley, where you can spend each day practicing your favorite adventure sport, or trying out a different one: paragliding, kayaking, freeflying, mountain biking, wingsuiting, freediving, canyoning, downhill longboarding, speedflying, caving and that's not even the whole menu. Adventure Boogie is exactly that.

Since during Adventure Boogie there's multiple activities happening all over this huge magical valley all at once, we had to be smart about spreading out our manpower. For the project we had a three-man groundcrew running around following the different adventures taking place, an fpv drone pilot and a dedicated editor who would continually collect everyone's footage and edit it on location to have a video to show by the end of every week. Meanwhile we needed a specialised camera crew to film activities taking place in the sky like skydiving, paragliding, speedflying and wingsuit base. Koen was supported in doing the aerial video work by multiple members of his skydiving/airsports network who were keen to help out getting any extra footage needed.
